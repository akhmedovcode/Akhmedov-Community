Prototype Tactical FPS

Strike Ops

A browser-based 3D first-person shooter inspired by competitive objective shooters. Fight across urban, industrial, and desert arenas with weapon recoil, ADS, grenades, bomb/hostage objectives, dynamic bots, ragdoll-style takedowns, and a lobby-style rules setup.

Gameplay Features

  • Two teams with rotating spawns and round-based objectives.
  • Rifle, pistol, sniper, and grenade loadout with reloads and switching.
  • ADS zoom, recoil kick, spread bloom, and damage falloff.
  • Bot callouts, flanking behavior, and difficulty scaling.

Map Design

  • Three handcrafted arenas with lanes, choke points, and bomb zones.
  • Balanced spawn protection and multiple attack paths.
  • Dynamic lighting, shadow casting, fog, and atmosphere.
  • Physics-based collision, projectile simulation, and simple ragdolls.

Left click to fire and right click to aim down sights. Press E near objective markers to plant, defuse, or rescue. Hold Shift to sprint, C to crouch, and R to reload. Use 1-4 to swap between pistol, rifle, sniper, and grenades. Bots react to sound, line of sight, and objective pressure.
